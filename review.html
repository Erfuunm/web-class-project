Perfect stay in every way!

The Ghasr Hotel exceeded all our expectations. From the moment we arrived, the staff was incredibly welcoming and attentive. Our room was spacious, clean. The bed was so comfortable we slept like babies every night. The location couldn't be better - right in the heart of Mashhad but surprisingly quiet. We especially enjoyed the rooftop bar with its spectacular city views. Will definitely be returning on our next trip to Mashhad!

Good but not exceptional

The Ghasr is a solid choice but didn't quite live up to the hype for us. The positives: excellent location, comfortable beds, and clean rooms. However, we found the service to be somewhat impersonal compared to other luxury hotels we've stayed at. Our room, while nice, was smaller than expected for the price. The hotel is good, but at this price point, we expected more personalized service and larger accommodations.
