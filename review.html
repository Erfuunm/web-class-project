Perfect stay in every way!

The Grand Plaza Hotel exceeded all our expectations. From the moment we arrived, the staff was incredibly welcoming and attentive. Our room was spacious, clean, and had a breathtaking view of Central Park. The bed was so comfortable we slept like babies every night. The location couldn't be better - right in the heart of Manhattan but surprisingly quiet. We especially enjoyed the rooftop bar with its spectacular city views. Will definitely be returning on our next trip to NYC!

Great hotel with minor issues

Overall a very good experience at the Grand Plaza. The location is perfect for exploring Manhattan, and the rooms are well-appointed. The staff was professional and helpful throughout our stay. Two small complaints: 1) The WiFi was occasionally spotty in our room, and 2) The breakfast buffet, while delicious, is quite expensive. These minor issues didn't detract significantly from our enjoyment, and we would certainly consider staying here again.

Good but not exceptional

The Grand Plaza is a solid choice but didn't quite live up to the hype for us. The positives: excellent location, comfortable beds, and clean rooms. However, we found the service to be somewhat impersonal compared to other luxury hotels we've stayed at. Our room, while nice, was smaller than expected for the price. The hotel is good, but at this price point, we expected more personalized service and larger accommodations.
